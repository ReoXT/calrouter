Frequently Asked Questions

search
Q How is billable usage calculated? expand_more
Usage is calculated based on the number of unique webhook events received by our ingress endpoints. Retries and internal routing steps do not count towards your monthly quota. We offer a generous free tier of 10k events/month.
Q Can I use a custom domain for my webhook URLs? expand_more
Yes, custom domains (CNAME) are available on the Pro and Enterprise plans. You can configure this in the Settings > Domains section of your dashboard. SSL certificates are automatically managed and renewed by us.
Q What is the maximum payload size supported? expand_more
The standard maximum payload size is 5MB for JSON bodies. If you need to process larger payloads (up to 25MB), please contact support to enable High Volume processing on your account.
Q How long are logs retained? expand_more
Log retention varies by plan. Free tier includes 3 days of retention, Starter includes 30 days, and Pro/Enterprise plans include 90 days to 1 year of searchable logs.
